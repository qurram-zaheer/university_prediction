Find people who got a reject from UTD for Computer Science

Find students who have been admitted and rejected for Fall 2021 / Fall 2020 and Spring 2022 / Spring 2021. See the average scores like SAT,GRE,GMAT,ACT & IELTS/TOEFL of students who have been admitted. Confused how much you should aim to get an admit in a University? Choose a University, for eg: UTD (University of Texas at Dallas) and your interested course, for eg: Computer Science with Level - Master's or Bachelor’s and you will find the profile of students that have received an admit or a reject in the past. Connect with them on our mobile app to get guidance on preparing for the application process.

How to Use Admits/Rejects to your benefit - University of Texas at Dallas

1. Don’t just look at the total scores, take a look at the Section Scores too. For Eg: Quants score & Verbal Scores for GRE and for SAT, look at the Writing & Language Section scores & Mathematics Score.

2. Take a look at their profile - what is their internship/work experience, what are their skills, achievements.

3. Take a look at the other Universities that they applied to. It may be helpful to look at their University List to check if you can apply there as well.

4. Interact with them to better understand their profile and check what’s missing from yours.
